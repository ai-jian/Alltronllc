About Us

ALLTRON is an electronic recycling company dedicated to protecting your privacy, and our environment. We pride ourselves on our commitment to providing our clients with electronic disposal and worry-free data security, with dependable customer service at each stage of the process.


You can count on our professionals to design and verify your custom recycling solutions. We have a team for each step of the disposal process to ensure that we uphold the highest standards for safety, security, and environmental sustainability.

  • We achieve environmental goals by relying on our sustainability experts.
  • All regulatory requirements are verified by compliance experts.
  • Optimal freight strategies are determined by our knowledgeable logistics team.
  • A team of operations experts devise the most effective way to implement your plan.
  • Our ITAD experts help you maximize the value of your assets by determining the best course of action for repurposing each item.

We work closely with each client to finalize each step of the process, ensuring that all of the details are ironed out prior to execution. We are proud to include our clients in this process to give them the peace of mind of knowing that their data is secure, and their electronics are disposed of safely and responsibly.
