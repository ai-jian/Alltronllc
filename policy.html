Policy

The environmental, health, and safety policies that guide our operations are a vital component of reducing the impact that our activities, services, and products have on the environment.


We are committed to:

  • Taking steps to prevent pollution, illness, and injuries
  • Ensuring that our facilities are clean, safe, and comply with regulatory requirements.
  • Providing our customers with customized solutions by thoroughly understanding their needs and expectations.
  • Adhering to a responsible reuse, refurbish, recovery, and disposal approach.
  • Doing our due diligence to protect our environment, and continually improving our process by regularly evaluating and reducing our environmental impact.
  • Making decisions that prioritize positive impacts on the environment, quality of life, and the health and safety of our team.
  • Continuously training our staff on the importance of having a positive environmental impact, and maintaining a safe, healthy work environment for our staff and our visitors.
  • Working efficiently to sort, consolidate, and manage material to improve productivity.
  • Examining and revising these policies as necessary to ensure that they remain both relevant and appropriate.
