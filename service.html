Service

Recycling

We will work with you directly to address your unique needs, and to have your electronic devices recycled quickly and efficiently.

The average time it takes for us to schedule and pick up your equipment is less than a week from your initial inquiry and request. In some cases, we are able to pick up items within 24 hours for clients who require expedited services. In order to take care of your electronics safely, our team will arrive at your facility on the day of pick-up, and remove and disconnect the items directly from the spot where they were previously connected.

Data Destruction

Your privacy and protection is our priority. We are proud to provide our clients with safe and reliable NAID AAA data destruction services.

All electronic devices containing data are sent through a thorough destruction process the moment they enter our facility. To give our clients peace of mind, we ensure that their data has been properly destroyed in compliance with government regulations.

Liquidation

If you need to clear out obsolete or unwanted office equipment, our team will save you time, money, and frustration with quick and efficient liquidation services.

All of your old equipment will be discarded by our team of experts with speed and efficiency, without causing any interruption to the flow of business or daily operations. We have the tools and resources needed to get the job done quickly and efficiently.

Whether your business is moving locations, upgrading equipment, or downsizing, we will ensure that your data and devices are safely and effectively disposed of. Our all-inclusive services save you the time and hassle of relying on multiple companies to thoroughly liquidate your obsolete equipment.
